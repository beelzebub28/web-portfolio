About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Listher Gonzales, mathematics degree holder from De La Salle University and a web developer student from Zuitt Learning Institute. I decided to shift career after four and a half years of working as a data analyst.

After my stint with ISS, I became a Health and Benefits Analyst for Willis Towers Watson (WTW) where we contribute to core health and benefits projects involving the design and management of group medical, dental and vision benefits.
